Kaizen: Shadow of the Regent – Beat-by-Beat Blueprint
Prologue / Act 1 – The Fall of the Dojo (~1 hour)

Area: Mountain Dojo & Village Outskirts
Gameplay Focus: Tutorial (basic melee, parry, movement)
Abilities Unlocked:

Spirit Strike (first magic enhancement)

Story Beats:

Intro cinematic: Kaizen training with Masahiro, learning swordsmanship and basic magic

Kurogane orders a public execution in nearby village

Masahiro attempts to stop the execution peacefully

Kaizen witnesses Masahiro executed by Kurogane (dramatic, emotionally heavy cutscene)

Kaizen vows revenge

Mini-Boss:

Executioner Foreman – teaches parry and riposte mechanics

Optional Side / Exploration:

Villagers hint at Kurogane’s regime

Hidden dojo scrolls explain Masahiro’s philosophy and early lore

Player Choices:

Explore village or proceed immediately to Kurogane’s forces

Interacting with villagers may reveal a hidden shortcut or minor combat advantage

Act 2 – The Tyrant’s Reach (~4 hours)

Area: Towns, Temples, Abandoned Villages
Gameplay Focus: Guided progression + exploration

Ability Unlocks (Story / Purchasable):

Echo Step (story progression, mobility + combo)

Blade Ward (story progression, parry defense)

Optional Weapons / Magic Scrolls (purchasable at stores; increases combo options, attack speed, or minor magical effects)

Mini-Bosses:

Elite Guard Captain – moderate difficulty, no story tie

Corrupted Samurai – lightly story-connected (previously loyal to Masahiro’s order, now forced to serve Kurogane)

Story Mini-Boss #1 – Masahiro’s Former Student – high difficulty, emotionally charged fight; tests advanced parry + Echo Step combos

Story Beats:

Kaizen travels across villages, fights Kurogane’s forces

Discovers Masahiro’s notes, hidden letters, and lore rooms

Villager interactions reveal hidden truths about Kurogane and his magical enhancements

Player choices:

Spare or kill enemies

Help or ignore villagers

These choices affect abilities unlocked, side quests, and dialogue options

Optional Side Quests:

Retrieve stolen village relics

Protect villagers during skirmishes

Collect Masahiro’s lost scrolls for unlockable abilities

Puzzles / Optional Challenges:

Locked doors / shrines requiring Spirit Strike + Echo Step combination

Hidden treasures in temple areas

Act 3 – The Tyrant’s Domain (~3 hours)

Area: Kurogane’s Fortress / Capital City
Gameplay Focus: High-stakes combat, mini-boss escalation, pre-final boss challenge

Ability Unlocks (Story):

Phantom Slash (spectral melee attack for multi-enemy combat)

Soul Resonance (buffs parry timing and melee damage)

Mini-Bosses:

Magical-Enchanted Guard – demonstrates magical enhancements, forces use of Blade Ward

Optional Combat Mini-Bosses – teach mastery of Phantom Slash in multi-enemy situations

Story Mini-Boss #2 – Kurogane’s Lieutenant / Enforcer – high difficulty, personally tied to Masahiro’s death, incorporates both melee and magic-enhanced attacks

Story Beats:

Kaizen navigates the fortress, encountering magical traps, elite guards, and remnants of Kurogane’s inner circle

Hidden lore rooms provide Kurogane’s backstory, magical enhancements, and insight into Masahiro’s philosophy

Player choices continue to influence:

Ability upgrades

Side quests completion

Mini-boss difficulty and behavior

Optional Side Quests / Exploration:

Rescue civilians / freed prisoners

Solve puzzles using Phantom Slash and Soul Resonance to reach hidden rooms

Collect artifacts that improve combat abilities

Final Boss – Lord Kurogane (~1 hour)

Arena: Throne Room

Burning braziers, torn banners, magical-enhanced architecture

Kurogane uses heavy sword attacks and magic-enhanced melee combos

Fight Phases:

Phase 1 – Tyrant: Heavy melee attacks, teaches pattern recognition and parry mastery

Phase 2 – Despot: Adds magical enhancements, area strikes, elite guards summoned

Phase 3 – Monster: Removes armor, aggressive melee-magic combo attacks, full challenge of all unlocked abilities

Story Beats:

Kurogane taunts Kaizen about Masahiro’s death:

“Your mentor was weak. He should have obeyed.”

Dialogue varies depending on completed side quests, optional lore, and previous choices

Player Choices / Endings:

Execution Ending: Kill Kurogane, end tyranny

Mercy Ending: Spare Kurogane, exile him (hard/optional)

Legacy Ending: Dismantle Kurogane’s forces, rebuild the land

Player choices affect epilogue scenes, survivor outcomes, and future ability availability

Epilogue / Resolution (~1 hour)

Kaizen reflects on Masahiro’s teachings and personal growth

Scenes vary depending on endings:

Cities rebuilding

Allies surviving or lost

Kaizen walking away alone or taking a leadership role

Optional playable character appearances (Ayame / Raizo) showing consequences of Kaizen’s actions

Game Flow Summary

Act 1 – Tutorial / Emotional Hook – Kaizen witnesses Masahiro’s death, learns basic combat and Spirit Strike

Act 2 – Tyrant’s Reach – Guided exploration, unlock Echo Step + Blade Ward, first story-connected mini-boss, optional side quests and lore

Act 3 – Tyrant’s Domain – Unlock Phantom Slash + Soul Resonance, mini-boss escalation, optional exploration, story mini-boss #2

Final Boss – Lord Kurogane – Multi-phase combat, story culmination, multiple endings

Epilogue – Player choices reflected in world, survivors, and Kaizen’s personal growth
